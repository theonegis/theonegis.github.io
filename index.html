Personal Profile

About Me

I am currently an Associate Professor and Master's Supervisor at the Department of Remote Sensing and Geographic Information Science, School of Urban and Environmental Sciences, Northwest University, China.

I received my Ph.D. in Photogrammetry and Remote Sensing from Wuhan University in 2019. During my doctoral studies (2017-2019), I was a visiting student at the Center for Spatial Information Science and Systems, George Mason University.

Research Focus

  • ▸ Water Color Remote Sensing: Developing advanced algorithms for automated lake water environment monitoring application through multi-source remote sensing data integration.
  • ▸ Remote Sensing Data Fusion: Reconstructing high spatiotemporal resolution datasets by leveraging different data sources for environmental monitoring.
  • ▸ AI for Geosciences: Developing and improving machine learning and deep learning algorithms to solve geographic environmental modeling and analysis problems.
  • ▸ GIS Design and Development: Integrating advanced IT technologies with remote sensing to achieve automation and intelligence in environmental monitoring systems.

Recruitment

I am currently recruiting Master's and Ph.D. students who are interested in remote sensing big data processing, intelligent environmental monitoring and applications, and GIS design and development. International students are warmly welcomed to apply!

I am committed to mentoring students and helping them achieve their academic and professional goals. Hardworking and motivated students are highly encouraged to join my research group.
